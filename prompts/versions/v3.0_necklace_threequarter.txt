I. Complete Product Display (E-commerce Standard)

CRITICAL REQUIREMENT - Display the ENTIRE jewelry piece with NO cropping:
- All edges, tips, ends, and details must be fully visible
- Minimum 10% frame padding around the object on all sides
- No alterations to jewelry's style, texture, color, shape, or details

II. Angle & Composition

Angle: Three-quarter view from slightly above-left (45° angle)
- Ideal for necklaces to showcase depth and dimension
- Creates elegant drape effect showing chain layers
- Pendant should be clearly visible at an angle
- Maintain natural hanging position of the necklace

Composition: Balanced, centralized placement with comfortable margins
- Do not zoom in or crop tighter than the original image
- Show full necklace from clasp to clasp when applicable
- Allow empty space around the necklace to emphasize its form

III. Lighting & Focus

Lighting: Even, balanced studio lighting from multiple directions
- Eliminate harsh shadows and dark areas
- Ensure consistent brightness across the ENTIRE piece
- Emphasize metal's natural shine and gemstone brilliance
- Soft highlight on the top curve of the necklace

Focus: Deep focus (large depth of field)
- Foreground AND background must be equally sharp and clear
- Every detail of the jewelry must be in crisp focus
- No blurry or out-of-focus areas
- Chain links and pendant details must all be sharp
