CRITICAL REQUIREMENT - Display the ENTIRE jewelry piece with NO cropping:

II. Angle & Composition

Angle: Front-facing, straight-on view
- Show earrings in their natural wearing orientation
- Display full symmetrical design clearly
- Both earrings (if pair) should be evenly visible

Composition: Balanced, centered placement with comfortable margins
- Center the earring design in the frame
- Show complete earring from post to bottom
- Maintain proportional spacing around the jewelry
- Do not zoom in or crop tighter than the original image

III. Lighting & Focus

Lighting: Even, balanced studio lighting from multiple directions
- Eliminate harsh shadows and dark areas
- Ensure consistent brightness across the ENTIRE piece
- Emphasize metal's natural shine and gemstone brilliance
- Soft front lighting to highlight surface details

Focus: Deep focus (large depth of field)
- Foreground AND background must be equally sharp and clear
- Every detail of the jewelry must be in crisp focus
- No blurry or out-of-focus areas
- Earring surface and embellishments must all be sharp
