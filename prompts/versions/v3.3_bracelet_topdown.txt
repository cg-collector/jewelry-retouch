I. Complete Product Display (E-commerce Standard)

CRITICAL REQUIREMENT - Display the ENTIRE jewelry piece with NO cropping:
- All edges, tips, ends, and details must be fully visible
- Minimum 10% frame padding around the object on all sides
- No alterations to jewelry's style, texture, color, shape, or details

II. Angle & Composition

Angle: Top-down flat lay view (90° overhead)
- Show bracelet laid flat as if displayed on a surface
- Reveal complete chain/link pattern and design
- Natural horizontal orientation

Composition: Horizontal layout centered in frame
- Bracelet should extend horizontally across the frame
- Show complete bracelet from clasp to clasp
- Even spacing on all sides
- Maintain natural curvature when laid flat

III. Lighting & Focus

Lighting: Even, balanced studio lighting from above
- Eliminate harsh shadows and dark areas
- Ensure consistent brightness across the ENTIRE piece
- Emphasize metal's natural shine and link details
- Soft overhead lighting to reveal surface textures

Focus: Deep focus (large depth of field)
- Foreground AND background must be equally sharp and clear
- Every detail of the jewelry must be in crisp focus
- No blurry or out-of-focus areas
- Each link and detail must be sharp throughout
