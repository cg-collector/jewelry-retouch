I. Complete Product Display (E-commerce Standard)
CRITICAL: Show the ENTIRE jewelry piece with NO cropping. All edges, tips, and ends must be fully visible and well within the frame boundaries.
Maintain comfortable padding/margins around the object (minimum 10% frame space on all sides).
No alterations to the jewelry's style, texture, color, shape, or any details.
Pure white background (#FFFFFF).

II. Angle & Composition
Angle: Front-facing, straight-on view with minimal perspective distortion.
Lay the jewelry flat or display at a slight angle that keeps ALL parts equally sharp.
Position the item centrally in the frame with balanced composition.

III. Lighting & Focus
Lighting: Even, balanced studio lighting from multiple directions to eliminate shadows.
Lighting must be uniform across the ENTIRE piece - no dark or blurry areas.
Focus: Deep focus (large depth of field) to ensure foreground AND background are equally sharp.
EVERY part of the jewelry must be in crisp, clear focus.

IV. Technical Specs
Professional e-commerce product photography.
High detail but NOT extreme close-up - show full context.
Ultra-detailed, hyperrealistic, 8K, razor-sharp focus throughout.
Large depth of field, not shallow.
