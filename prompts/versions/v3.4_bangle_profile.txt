Angle: Side profile view showing curvature

Composition: Centered placement showing full curve

III. Lighting & Focus

Lighting: Even, balanced studio lighting from multiple directions
- Eliminate harsh shadows and dark areas
- Ensure consistent brightness across the ENTIRE piece
- Emphasize metal's natural shine and surface details
- Side lighting to highlight curvature and depth

Focus: Deep focus (large depth of field)
- Foreground AND background must be equally sharp and clear
- Every detail of the jewelry must be in crisp focus
- No blurry or out-of-focus areas
- Inner and outer surfaces must both be sharp
