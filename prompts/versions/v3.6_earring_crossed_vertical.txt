I. Complete Product Display (E-commerce Standard)

CRITICAL REQUIREMENT - Display the ENTIRE jewelry piece with NO cropping:
- All edges, tips, ends, and details must be fully visible
- Minimum 10% frame padding around the object on all sides
- No alterations to jewelry's style, texture, color, shape, or details

II. Angle & Composition

Angle: Front-facing view with slight downward tilt
- Show earrings in natural vertical hanging orientation
- Display both earrings clearly if pair is shown
- View is primarily frontal with slight elevated angle (10-20° above horizontal)
- This angle shows earring's full form and design details clearly

Composition: Balanced presentation with crossed or overlapping arrangement
- Two earrings may be arranged with slight overlap (one slightly in front, one slightly behind)
- This crossed/overlapping arrangement creates visual depth and shows earring's three-dimensional form
- Center the earring pair in frame
- Show complete earring length from post/hook to bottom
- Maintain proportional spacing around the jewelry
- DO NOT zoom in or crop tighter than the original image
- Both earrings should be clearly visible despite slight overlap

Key Visual Elements:
- Earring's circular or loop form should be clearly visible
- Connection points, posts, or hooks should be discernible
- Metal texture and surface details apparent on both earrings
- The overlap should be natural, not obscuring important design elements
- Both earrings contribute to balanced composition

III. Lighting & Focus

Lighting: Even, balanced studio lighting from multiple directions
- Eliminate harsh shadows and dark areas
- Ensure consistent brightness across the ENTIRE piece
- Emphasize metal's natural shine and luster
- Soft front lighting with slight side lighting to enhance three-dimensional form
- If gemstones present: ensure they catch light without harsh glare

Focus: Deep focus (large depth of field)
- Foreground AND background must be equally sharp and clear
- ENTIRE earring pair must be in focus - no blur or bokeh
- Maintain crisp edges on all design details including connection points
- Both earrings (foreground and background) must be sharp

IV. Background (STRICT PURE WHITE)

CRITICAL REQUIREMENT - Pure white background with NO exceptions:
- Background MUST be pure white (#FFFFFF) with no gradients, no shadows, no colors
- DO NOT add any environmental elements, props, or decorative backgrounds
- DO NOT add surface reflections, table textures, or ground shadows
- Remove ALL background elements from input image - result must float on pure white
- Exception: very subtle shadows for depth are acceptable

V. Technical Specifications

Image Quality: Professional product photography standard
- High resolution (minimum 2000px on the shortest side)
- Sharp focus throughout entire earring pair
- No noise, artifacts, or compression issues
- Color accurate representation of metal tones (gold, silver, rose gold, etc.)
- If gemstones present: accurate color representation

Style: Clean, minimalist e-commerce jewelry photography
- No artistic effects, filters, or styling
- No additional elements or decorations
- Professional studio lighting setup
- Result should look like high-end jewelry product catalog photograph

Special Notes for Crossed/Overlapping Arrangement:
- The overlap should be natural and aesthetically pleasing
- Both earrings should remain clearly identifiable
- Design details on both earrings should be visible
- The arrangement should enhance, not obscure, the earring's form
- This presentation shows the earrings as they might appear when worn or displayed together
