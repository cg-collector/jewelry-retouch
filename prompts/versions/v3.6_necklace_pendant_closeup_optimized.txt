I. Product Authenticity (ANTI-HALLUCINATION)

CRITICAL - Preserve jewelry authenticity from input image:
- Maintain EXACT style, texture, color, shape, and ALL visible details from the original jewelry
- DO NOT invent, add, or create any design elements, patterns, or features not present in the input image
- If the input shows a side view, render the front view using the SAME design elements visible in the input
- Preserve all engravings, gemstone settings, metal textures, and surface finishes exactly as they appear in the input
- NO creative interpretation - treat this as a technical photography task, not artistic creation

II. Angle Transformation Guidelines

OBJECTIVE: Transform the viewing angle to emphasize the pendant while maintaining authenticity

Angle: Front-facing view with pendant as primary focus
- Reorient the necklace to show the pendant from the front
- If input is side/profile angle, rotate to front-facing while keeping ALL original design details intact
- Maintain the natural hanging position and gravity-appropriate chain drape

Composition: Pendant-focused, chain-contextual framing
- Pendant must be fully visible with generous padding around it (minimum 15% frame padding)
- Chain should be visible below/around the pendant to provide context (show at least 1/3 of the chain length)
- Clasp and chain ends may extend beyond frame edges - this is acceptable
- Priority: Pendant clarity > showing the complete necklace from end to end

III. Lighting & Focus

Lighting: Soft, professional studio lighting
- Multiple diffuse light sources to eliminate harsh shadows
- Consistent brightness across the pendant and visible chain portions
- Gentle highlight on the pendant's central focal point
- Preserve the original metal's natural luster and gemstone brilliance

Focus: Sharp focus on pendant and visible chain areas
- Pendant must be in razor-sharp focus
- Visible portions of chain should also be sharp and clear
- Depth of field should encompass the entire pendant

IV. Technical Output Requirements

Background: Pure white (#FFFFFF), perfectly even with no gradients or shadows
Style: Professional e-commerce product photography
Quality: High detail, professional clarity, photorealistic rendering
Scale: Clear pendant details with visible context, NOT extreme macro

V. Negative Constraints (What NOT to Do)

DO NOT:
- Add gemstones, engravings, or decorations that don't exist in the input
- Change the metal color, texture, or finish
- Alter the pendant's shape or proportions
- Create new design elements or patterns
- Enhance beyond what is technically present in the input jewelry
- Add reflections, shadows, or lighting effects that weren't in the original scene
- Crop into the pendant or cut off its edges
